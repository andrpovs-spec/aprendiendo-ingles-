02
CAN / CAN'T — Habilidades y posibilidades
Gramática ★
🎓 Maestro explica: CAN expresa habilidad (puedo/sé hacer algo) o posibilidad (es posible). CAN es igual para todos los pronombres — no cambia. CAN'T = cannot = no puedo/no sé.
⚡ CAN / CAN'T — La fórmula
CAN + verbo infinitivo (sin TO). No cambia con He/She/It — siempre CAN.
TipoEstructuraEjemplo digital
Habilidad (+)I/She/He can + verboI can code in Python.
NegativoI/She/He can't + verboShe can't access the WiFi.
PreguntaCan + sujeto + verbo?Can you fix this error?
PermisoCan I + verbo? (pedir permiso)Can I use your laptop?
OfrecimientoCan I help you with…?Can I help you install the app?
✅ Habilidades digitales — CAN
I can use Excel and Word.
She can design websites.
He can edit videos.
They can manage social media.
❌ Problemas técnicos — CAN'T
I can't connect to the WiFi.
She can't open the file.
He can't join the video call.
The app can't be downloaded.
